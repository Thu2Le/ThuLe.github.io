About Me

My name is Thu (pronounced like the number) Le. I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in Biology, minoring in Anthropology from the University of Vermont. My first introduction to R programming was in college while taking Ecology. With a newfound interest in data analytics, I self taught SQL, Tableau, and R. Structural thinking have alway been a part of my career from being an undergraduate Biologist to beginner analyst. From asking questions to form hypotheses, to thinking of methods to test the hypotheses, and finally drawing a conclusion to make data-driven decision making.
